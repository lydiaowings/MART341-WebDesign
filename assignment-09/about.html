Best Star Trek Characters

As ranked by Lydia, one from each series I've seen

Spock

Series: TOS (The Original Series)
Species: Half Vulcan, Half Human
Role: Science Officer and First Officer
Why I Love: A mix of logic and emotion, Spock shows us as well as his fellow characters what it means to be human while also valuing science and rational thought.
Spock is the reason I minored in Ethics. His friendships make me happy cry.

Geordi LaForge

Series: TNG (The Next Generation)
Species: Human
Role: Chief Engineer
Why I Love: LeVar Burton (actor) brings a dash of Reading Rainbow to this character.
Geordi spends his time saving the Enterprise, befriending the android he works alongside, and mentoring the child prodigy onboard.

Julian Bashir

Series: DS9 (Deep Space Nine)
Species: Genetically Enhanced Human
Role: Chief Medical Officer (doctor)
Why I Love: Introduced as a young doctor who excelled in everything he tries, Bashir was orginally painted as annoying and admittedly creepy.
As we saw the character grow and learn, his history of illegal genetic enhancement throws a wrench in his self image.
Grappling to accept himself and to be seen as legitimate, he shows strength and humility can combine without shame.

B'Elanna Torres

Series: VOY (Voyager)
Species: Half human, Half Klingon
Role: Chief Engineer
Why I Love: Originally a hot-headed member of a terrorist organization, B'Elanna learns to accept Starfleet guidelines and finds her sensitive side along the way.
One of the first women I saw on TV in a powerful and important STEM role, B'Elanna taught young girls that being smart and interested in male-dominated fields was okay.

Hoshi Sato

Series: ENT (Enterprise)
Species: Human
Role: Communications Officer
Why I Love: An exo-linguist, Hoshi is responsible for communicating with completely new species in their native language.
She is responsible for programming the universal translator and ensuring diplomatic communications take place.
Stepping outside of her comfort zone, she takes on the responsiblity of representing the entire crew and quickly learning new syntax and grammar that ultimately saves lives.

Saru

Series: DISCO (Discovery)
Species: Kelpien
Role: First Officer
Why I Love: Born on a pre-warp planet, Saru fought his anxious instincts to communicate with and join an interstellar organization nobody on his planet knew existed.
Being thrust into the role of captain in alternate universes and timelines, Saru commands his crew with level-headed diligence after overcoming his extreme anxiety.
He also walks on hooved tip-toes, which I just think is fun.

How to Pickle Any Veggie

Ingredients

  • any vegetable
  • apple cider vinegar
  • water
  • brown sugar
  • salt (any kind)

Directions

Fill a jar with chopped or diced vegetables.
Add a mixture of 70% apple cider vinegar and 30% water until the jar is almost full.
Add a tablespoon of brown sugar and about two tablespoons of salt.
Shake the jar. Heat if desired. Store in a cool place.
